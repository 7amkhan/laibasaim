I LOVE YOU SO MUCH

Meri Jaan, Aaj dil mein itna gehra ehsaas hei ke shayad lafzon mein bayan hi na ho sake, lekin phir bhe tumhary liye mere jazbaat likhne ki koshish kar raha hoon. Tum mery liye sirf ek shakhs nahi ho, tum meri saanson ki hawa ho, mery dil ki dharkan ho, meri har khushi aur gham ki saathi ho. Tumhary bina zindagi adhoori si lagti hai, jaisy raat bina chaand ke, phool bina khushboo ke. Har pal tumhary saath bitana, tumhari muskaan dekhna, tumhari aawaz sunna yehi meri sabse bari khwahish hai. Tumhary pyaar ne mujhy itna strong banaya hai, itna complete mehsoos karaya hai ke main kabhi soch bhi nahi sakta tha. Tumhary ishq ne mere andar ek naya junoon bhar diya hai, har mushkil aasan lagne lagti hai jab tum saath hoti ho. Meri jaan, tum samajhti ho na? Tumhare liye mera pyaar lafzon se bhe zyada hai. Kabhi kabhi lagta hai ke main itna pyaar kar sakta hoon, itna de sakta hoon, lekin phir bhi kam lagta hai. Kyunki tumhara ehsaas, tumhara saath, tumhari mohabbat inky aage meri har koshish chhoti par jaati hai. Tum meri pehli aur aakhri mohabbat ho, meri har duaa mein shaamil ho, meri har khwaish ka saaz ho. Chahy jitni bhi dooriyan ho, main hamesha tumhary saath hoon. Tumhary pyaar ne mujhe itna rich kar diya hai ke ab main kisi aur ka intezaar nahi kar sakta. Tum hi ho meri manzil, tum hi ho meri raah. Bas itna kehna chahta hoon "I LOVE YOU" is pori kainaat se zyada, "I ADORE YOU" se bhi pary, tumhary liye mere dil mein ek aisi duniya hai jahan sirf tum rehti ho. Hamesha mere saath rehna, meri jaan.
