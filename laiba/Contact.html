YOU MEAN EVERTHING TO ME

Meri Jaan, Tum mere liye sirf ek shaks nahi ho, tum meri saanson ki har ek saans ho, meri har khwahish ka sarmaya ho, aur meri zindagi ka sabse khoobsurat ehsaas ho. Tumhary bina ye zindagi adhoori si lagti hai, jaisy kisi kitaab mein sirf khali safoon ka majmooha ho. Tum mery dil ki dharkan ho, meri har muskaan ki wajah ho, aur har aansuon ka sahara ho. Tumhari mohabbat ne mujhy itna strong banaya hai ke main har mushkil ka saamna kar sakoon, kyun ky mjhy pata hai ke tum mery saath ho. Tumhari ek nazar, ek choti si muskurahat, ya bas ek jhalak ki nazar hi mujhe itni himmat de deti hai ke main duniya ki har takleef ko bhool jaata hoon. Tumhary bina ye zindagi be-maainy hai, jaisy raat bina chaand ke, phool bina khushboo ke. Meri jaan, tum mery liye woh pyaar ho jo har dard ko bhula deta hai, woh aasra jo har andhery mein roshni ban kar aata hai. Tumhari yaadon mein hi main khoya rehta hoon, har pal tumhary saath bitana chahta hoon. Tumhary saath guzara har lamha mere liye ek khazana hai, ek aisa gehna jo main hamesha apne dil mein mehfooz rakhoonga. Tumhari har ada, har baat, har ehsaas mere liye anmol hai. Main chahta hoon ke tum hamesha mere saath raho, mere saath hanso, mere saath ro lo, aur mere saath har ek pal ko jiyo. Kyun ky tumhary bina to ye zindagi kuch bhi nahi. Tum ho to main hoon, tumhari mohabbat hi meri zindagi ka maqsad hai.
